# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-04-21

This Privacy Policy explains how the HabitTracker iOS application (the “**App**”) handles information when you use it. The App is published by an individual developer - Sergey Frolov (“**we**”, “**us**”, “**our**”).

If you have questions, contact us at **mooscle@gmail.com**.

## 1. What this policy covers
This policy describes:
- What information may be collected when you use the App
- How we use and share that information
- The third-party services (SDKs) we use

## 2. App data you enter
The App stores information you enter, such as habits, schedules, reminders, notes, and preferences (“**App Data**”).

**No iCloud sync:** App Data is stored locally on your device (we do not provide iCloud sync at this time). If you delete the App, your App Data may be deleted from your device.

## 3. Information collected automatically (analytics/diagnostics)
When you use the App, we and our third-party providers may collect limited technical information such as:
- Device and app information (device model, OS version, app version, language/region)
- Usage and interaction data (e.g., which screens/features are used, session metrics)
- Diagnostics data and crash reports (including stack traces and event logs)

This information helps us operate, analyze, and improve the App.

## 4. Subscriptions and purchases
The App offers paid subscriptions.

Purchases are processed by **Apple’s App Store / In‑App Purchase system**. We do not collect your full payment card details.

To manage subscriptions and measure purchase performance, we use **Adapty**, which may process identifiers and purchase-related events such as:
- Subscription status (active/expired), product identifiers, purchase timestamps
- Device/app identifiers used to attribute purchases and diagnose subscription issues
- Paywall and offer interactions (for example, which paywall was shown)

## 5. Third-party services we use
We use the following third-party services:

- **Sentry** (crash reporting / diagnostics): helps us detect, investigate, and fix errors and stability issues.
- **Google Analytics** (analytics): helps us understand App usage and improve features.
- **Amplitude** (product analytics): helps us understand how features are used and improve the user experience.
- **Yandex AppMetrica** (analytics): helps us measure usage and performance.
- **Adapty** (subscriptions infrastructure and analytics): helps us manage paywalls, purchases, subscription status, and purchase-related performance.

These providers process information according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies for details on what they collect, how they use it, and how to exercise your rights with them.

## 6. How we use information
We use information to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Monitor performance, troubleshoot issues, and fix bugs
- Understand usage to improve features and usability
- Operate subscriptions, paywalls, and validate subscription status
- Respond to support requests

## 7. How we share information
We do not sell your personal information.

We share information:
- With the third-party services listed above to operate analytics, diagnostics, and subscriptions infrastructure
- If required by law or valid legal process
- To protect the security and integrity of the App and our users

## 8. Data retention
- **App Data** is stored locally on your device.
- Analytics/diagnostics/subscription-related data is retained according to the third-party providers’ retention settings and policies.

## 9. Your choices
You may be able to limit some collection via:
- iOS privacy settings (where applicable)
- Any in-app toggles we provide for analytics/diagnostics (if available)
- Uninstalling the App (stops further collection)

To manage or cancel your subscription, use Apple’s subscription management:
- iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

## 10. Children’s privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, contact us at **mooscle@gmail.com**.

## 11. Security
We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 12. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above. Your continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the updated Policy.
